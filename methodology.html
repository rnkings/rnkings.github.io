How we work


Here at Square One Creative we work using the Agile development methodology.

What is Agile do you ask?


Agile is a way of working in sprints, increments and iterations allowing feedback along the way.

What does that mean for you?


This means that we show you the progress of our work along the way and we make changes frequently. This way you are able to play a part in the overall workflow and this ensures you will get the final product delivered based on your specs.
